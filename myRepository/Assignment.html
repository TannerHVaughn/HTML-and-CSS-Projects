Cats are sweet!

Cats are responsive just as much as dogs are; they can learn so many tricks and not be mean to everyone if you treat them with love and affection.

Cats love to snuggle!

Cats usually love to just lay near you and bask in the warmth your radiating. Some cats, though, like to do their own thing and just be around. Not necessiarily right on you, just in the room observing, judging.

Cats don't eat as much!

Most cats are a lot smaller than dogs and therefore, eat much less food. My dog has a problem with eating the cats's food when I'm at work. Point being, cats don't need to eat all of their food in one sitting.
